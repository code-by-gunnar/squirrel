Everything it does

Small app, surprisingly complete.

The features you'd actually use to keep subscriptions under control, and nothing you wouldn't.

Renewal reminders

A heads-up a set number of days before each renewal and again on the day — via ntfy, Telegram or email. Enable any combination.

Renewal calendar

A month view showing exactly when each subscription bills, so a heavy week never catches you out.

Spend reports

A ledger of every real charge, with the exchange rate saved at the time, charted as your actual monthly spend plus running totals.

Personal & Work contexts

Tag each subscription by area — Personal, Work, or your own — then switch the whole app to one. The dashboard, calendar and reports re-scope to just that context; "All" brings it back together.

Cancellations

Mark something cancelled and it stays usable until it expires, then bows out of your totals automatically.

Free-tier tracking

Flag the services you're on the free plan for. Kept for awareness, left out of your spend.

Multi-currency

Track subs in any currency; totals convert to your base with free daily ECB rates, no API key.

Install as an app

It's a PWA: add it to your phone's home screen for a native feel with a bottom nav and slide-up forms.

Import, export & backup

Bulk-import your subscriptions from a CSV — from another tracker or a spreadsheet — with a preview and backfilled history. Export as CSV, or take a full JSON backup you can restore anywhere. Your data, portable.

Install

Up and running in two minutes.

  1. Paste the stack

    Into TrueNAS "Custom App", Dockge, Portainer, or a compose.yaml file.

  2. Edit two secrets

    Set a password and a random session secret. That's the whole config.

  3. Deploy

    Open it at your NAS address. Your data lives in one tidy volume.

compose.yaml 
services:
  squirrel:
    image: ghcr.io/code-by-gunnar/squirrel:latest
    container_name: squirrel
    restart: unless-stopped
    ports:
      - "8480:3000"
    environment:
      APP_PASSWORD: "change-me"
      SESSION_SECRET: "a-long-random-string"
      BASE_CURRENCY: "GBP"
      TZ: "Europe/London"
    volumes:
      - squirrel-data:/app/data
volumes:
  squirrel-data:

That's the whole app — nothing else required. Notifications work out of the box via ntfy, Telegram or email. Want a fully self-hosted ntfy server too? It's an optional add-on; the setup guide on GitHub has the drop-in stack.
